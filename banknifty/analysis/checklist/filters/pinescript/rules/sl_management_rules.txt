STOP LOSS MANAGEMENT – BANKNIFTY (5 MIN)

RULE-1: CANDLE-BASED SL ONLY
❌ Fixed points SL (20/30 pts) NOT allowed
✅ SL always below/above structure candle

BUY TRADE:
- SL = Entry candle ka LOW ( + buffer )

SELL TRADE:
- SL = Entry candle ka HIGH ( + buffer )

BUFFER:
- 5–10 points (market volatility ke hisaab se)

------------------------------------------------

RULE-2: ENTRY CANDLE FILTER (MOST IMPORTANT)
❌ Agar entry candle ka size > previous 5 candles avg
→ NO TRADE (SL hunting candle hoti hai)

------------------------------------------------

RULE-3: HTF SL CONFIRMATION
- 15 min chart par:
  ❌ Agar SL level ke paas support/resistance ho
  → Trade SKIP

------------------------------------------------

RULE-4: FIRST PULLBACK ENTRY
❌ Direct breakout candle pe entry nahi
✅ Breakout → small pullback → entry

(Isse SL hit probability half ho jaati hai)

------------------------------------------------

RULE-5: TIME FILTER
❌ 9:15 – 9:25 (noise)
❌ 2:45 ke baad (random moves)

✅ Best time:
- 9:30 – 11:30
- 1:45 – 2:30

------------------------------------------------

RULE-6: MAX SL PER DAY
- Max 2 SL per day
- 2 SL hit → TRADING STOP

------------------------------------------------

RULE-7: MOVE TO BREAKEVEN
- Jab price = 1:1 RR
→ SL = Entry price

------------------------------------------------

GOLDEN RULE:
❗ SL hit = BAD TRADE nahi
❗ Rule break = BAD TRADE
