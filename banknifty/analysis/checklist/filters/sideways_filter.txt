SIDEWAYS MARKET FILTER – BANKNIFTY (5 MIN)

RULE-1: RANGE CHECK
- Last 20 candles ka high–low < 0.25% of price
→ SIDEWAYS (NO TRADE)

RULE-2: EMA COMPRESSION
- 20 EMA, 50 EMA, 100 EMA ek dusre ke paas ho
- Price EMAs ke beech me ho
→ SIDEWAYS

RULE-3: FAKE BREAKOUT ZONE
- Multiple wicks both sides
- Candle body small, wicks large
→ SIDEWAYS

RULE-4: VOLUME FILTER
- Current volume < 70% of 20-candle average
→ NO TRADE

RULE-5: HTF CONFIRMATION
- 15m / 30m chart bhi range me ho
→ NO TRADE

FINAL RULE:
- Agar 2 ya zyada rules TRUE ho
→ STRICT NO TRADE

NOTE:
- Sideways market = SL machine
- Best trades come after range breakout
